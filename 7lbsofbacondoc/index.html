7lbs. of Bacon Documentary

A year on "tour" with the band that says they "set music back 150 years". Our documentary follows the members of a Civil War re-enactment band as they record and support their newest album. Follow the campaign through Florida Civil War re-enacting and other non-traditional stops on an exciting musical journey.
