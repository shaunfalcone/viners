Rearrangement (Aναδιοργάνωση)

2018 Narrative Short • Producer: Ioannis Hansen

Greek language film, shot on location on the island of Cyprus.

When Leonidas (Makis Papadimitriou) pursues an home renovation contract, he quickly comes to find that his potential new client is very insistent that everything must be done according to her demands.
