Tertium Quid

2017 Narrative Short • Producers: Ioannis Hansen, Shaun Falcone

In a seedy bar, a mysterious woman holds court before her drunken congregation. Out back, unique and interesting world views are bought and sold on the black market. Into this world falls Gordon, a former minister who has lost his faith, and now hopes to shed his history and identity.

His search may lead all parties to the TERTIUM QUID: A mysterious 'third way' said to open itself to our awareness when there seem to be no answers to our questions.
